## What is the catastrophic issue?

Catastrophic issues are the issues that mean collar malfunctioning. Halo assumes such collars are dangerous for dogs wearing them and wants our users not to use them. Once we detect such an issue, we notify the user via a few channels:

1. The collar starts vibrating and blinking when put off the charger (seeCollar Actions).
2. A short description of the issue is displayed on theCollar Diagnosticscreen in the Halo mobile app.
3. A Braze Content Card is displayed on the My Map.

Note: we never mention the word 'catastrophic' for our users in the app, articles, and any other channels of communication. It can scare our users.

## What do we do with catastrophic issues?

- Warranty replacement (major flow)

After the user finds the issue, we encourage them to claim the warranty replacement. For this purpose warranty window is automatically extended: i.e. even if the warranty window expires, we allow them to replace collars with catastrophic issues.

Support article for users https://support.halocollar.com/hc/en-us/articles/5575769230103-What-to-Do-if-Collar-Diagnostic-Reports-an-Issue

- Reset issues

CSA has an option to Reset issues. It could be used in case after analyzing the Workbook details CSA thinks there might be a mistake or the issue was resolved by distant troubleshooting. After CSA resets the issue, the collar will stop vibration and blinking, and the mobile app will stop displaying the information about the issue. If afterward issue is detected again, all consequences will happen again.
