| HALO-4672 - Android: ME04-F01. Fence card Closed HALO-4674 - Android GOAL: Fence annotation on My Map (+centering) Closed |## User story

\> As an account owner, I want to view the selected fence with its card so that I can review available fence information and initiate fence management (if necessary). As a Halo account owner I want clearly see the map elements inside fence and where the warning zone begins so that I can double-check if required elements position meets my expectations.As a Halo business owner I want replace blue fence color with yellow so that add more brand to the app and user's recognition of the application.As a Halo account owner I want to view if the pets listen the fence details for which I opened so that I can understand if my dogs will be safe inside this fence.

User story Acceptance Criteria Select a fence and open its card Fence Card states Pets on Fence Card Selected fence with Protection Zone and Warning area Close Fence Card
