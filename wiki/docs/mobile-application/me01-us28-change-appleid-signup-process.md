| As of 30 Mar 2022 edited the ME01-US28-AC03 |# General description

We have a common issue when users sign up via AppleID: they get their user name assigned to the Apple Private Relay email address. Then, if they log in from a non-Apple device, they cannot (easily) because they have to type a ridiculous email address.

Can we change AppleID signup so that it (ideally) automatically uses their Apple-known email (AppleID) as their account email/ID - OR, at worst, ask them to type/confirm an email address to use for non-Apple logins?

## User Story

As Halo App Account owner I want to be aware that when I choose 'hide my email' option during sign up process with my Apple ID, I will be provided with email randomly generated by Apple Private Relay service.
