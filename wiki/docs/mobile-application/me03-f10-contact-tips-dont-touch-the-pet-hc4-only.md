Dog behavior specifics: During regular movement of the dog 'contact tips don't touch the pet' situation should never happen if the collar is installed properly. But if the user put super loosely not tight enough, then it can happen and 'ME03-F10' should be a solution to resolve the issue. We assume that this scenario mostly happen during the first 3 weeks of the product usage

Note: Also HC4 is for smaller dogs and the positioning for them is very important

General objectives:

1. to give the confidence in the product and give the user piece of mind
2. to give the feedback to user about the fact that the collar is put correctly on the dog as soon as the users put it on the dog, so that he/she doesn't let the dog go and the have to invite the dog go back

Solution objectives:

1. to eliminate the need to have a phone near the user while putting the collar on the dog
2. to avoid false positive

Positive scenario

The most common scenario of the product: 'the dog goes out in the morning'

- the user wakes up
- the user takes the collar from the charger
- the user puts the collar on the dog
- if the collar put on collar correctly, than he/she hears the audible feedback and let the dog go
- if the collar put on collar**incorrectly**, than he/she doesn't hear the audible feedback and should readjust the collar

Negative scenario

Accidental scenario: 'lose of contact by accident'

- the collar is put correctly on the dog
- the dog goes out
- the contact tips are no longer making contact

Purposeful scenario: 'collar removal at the end of the day'

- the user puts the collar on the dog at the morning
- the collar is ON
- 8 hours later I put it off
- the collar is not making with your dog anymore
