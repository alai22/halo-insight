# User story

\> As Halo app account owner, I want to be notified if my fence overlaps hazards such as other fences, building, road, water so I can adjust my fence to avoid overlapping.

| ME04-F06-AC10 | PreconditionsEdit Fence screen is opened.ORConfirm Fence screen is opened.If I zoom out, then the app should:Hover the new displaying parts of road and water with a required pattern.But only if this is part of the same piece of a road and water that is intersected. |Tech details

During validation of the fence, when it is changed Validation of intersections with objects on the map it is also necessary to calculate intersecting polygons (or define main buildings that are included in the fence)To avoid overlapping of one polygon on another, it is necessary to draw the overlapped zone and the non-overlapped zone separatelyIn case of an overlapped fence, we should hide the polygon of a regular fence and replace it with a patterned overlapped fence

Objects on the map can consist of several parts; for example, a road parcel can have two roads. To display this as a whole, for visualization purposes, they can be combined into one large polygon (Only the same type of polygons)If it is not possible to merge polygons (because they are very far away, for example), we leave them as they are
