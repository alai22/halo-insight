| HALO-20419 - FW+BE: Use vibration instead of static in case contact tips don't touch the pet Reopened |
| 28 May 2024 the draft user story is created |# User story

\> As Halo collar account owner I want the collar to send vibration feedback in case the contact tips don't touch the skin of the dog so that (1) I can use 'hand fence test' feature without the risk of getting static feedback and (2) I can be sure that my dog always get feedback/protected even in case the contact tips don't touch the pet skin.
